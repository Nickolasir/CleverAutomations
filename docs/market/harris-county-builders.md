## Executive Summary

The Greater Houston area is the largest new-construction housing market in the United States, with over 40,000 single-family permits issued annually across Harris, Fort Bend, Montgomery, Brazoria, and Galveston counties. Houston's lack of zoning, relatively affordable land, and sustained population growth (60,000+ net new residents/year) make it the ideal beachhead market for CleverHome.

This document catalogs 110+ active homebuilders in the Greater Houston market, organized into three tiers by scale and approach. Each tier requires a distinct sales strategy.

### Tier 1 Contact Strategy

1. **Identify Regional VP of Purchasing / VP of Operations** — These roles control vendor selection for Houston division.
2. **Target National Innovation/Technology teams** — Lennar (Miami), KB Home (LA), Meritage (Scottsdale) all have dedicated smart home decision-makers.
3. **Approach via GHBA (Greater Houston Builders Association)** — Attend monthly meetings, sponsor events. Annual Home & Outdoor Living Show is key.
4. **Offer pilot program**: 1 model home + 10 spec homes at reduced cost to prove install speed and buyer reception.
5. **Lead with ROI data**: Cost per home vs. upgrade revenue potential.
6. **Timeline**: 6–12 months from first meeting to first install for nationals. Budget cycles typically Q3/Q4 for following year.

1. **Direct outreach to Owner/President or VP of Sales** — These companies have 1-2 decision-makers, not procurement committees.
2. **Attend GHBA events and Texas Association of Builders** — Regional builders are deeply embedded in local associations.
3. **Offer "CleverHome Launch Partner" program**: First 5 regional builders get preferred pricing + co-marketing.
4. **Model home demo**: Install in their highest-traffic model for 90-day trial. Measure buyer engagement.
5. **Lead with simplicity**: 30-minute install, no specialized subcontractor needed.
6. **Timeline**: 3–6 months from first meeting to PO. Many can approve in a single meeting.

### Tier 3 Contact Strategy

1. **Direct to Owner/Founder** — Custom builders are often 1-5 person operations. The owner IS the decision-maker.
2. **Referrals from architects, interior designers, and AV integrators** — Custom builders rely on design partners.
3. **Install in one completed home as showcase** — Offer to install free in the builder's own home or personal office.
4. **Social media / Instagram presence** — Many custom builders in Heights/Montrose are Instagram-active. Co-create content.
5. **Lead with design + voice experience** — Custom buyers care about aesthetics and experience, not cost savings.
6. **Timeline**: 1–3 months. A single lunch meeting can close a deal.
